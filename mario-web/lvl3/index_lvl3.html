🌋 Level 3: Final Boss Battle ⚔️

Instructions:

  • 🕹️ Use arrow keys to move Mario
  • ⬆️ Press UP to jump
  • 🔥 Press F to drop a Fire Mushroom (shoot fireballs)
  • 💧 Press W to drop a Water Mushroom (shoot waterballs)
  • ➕ Press G to drop a Heal (increase energy)
  • ⭐ Press S to drop a Star (temporary invincibility & speed)
  • ⚠️ Defeat Bowser by shooting or jumping on him
  • 💥 Avoid Bowser’s attacks — survive and win!
  • 🏁 Reach the flag to rescue Princess Peach 👸 and Toad 🍄
  • 🔁 Press R to restart after Game Over 🔄
